    What is PGN? PGN (Portable Game Notation) is the standard text format for chess games. Every chess website and engine reads it. You can copy this text to share with someone, save it as a file to keep on your computer, or open it directly in Lichess to get free, professional-grade analysis from Stockfish — one of the strongest chess engines in the world.
